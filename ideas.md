# Portfolio Design - Dilani Darshika

## Selected Design Approach: Simple & Personal Student Portfolio

### Design Movement
**Clean Minimalism** - A straightforward, student-friendly aesthetic that's professional yet approachable. Focuses on clarity and ease of navigation.

### Core Principles
1. **Simplicity First**: Clean layouts, minimal decoration, focus on content
2. **Personal Touch**: Reflects a student's journey and learning
3. **Dark Blue Professionalism**: Deep blue conveys reliability without being overly corporate
4. **Easy to Navigate**: Clear sections and intuitive flow

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary Dark Blue**: #0F3A7D (main background, professional)
- **Secondary Blue**: #1A4B96 (interactive elements)
- **Accent Cyan**: #00D9FF (highlights, calls-to-action)
- **Neutral Grays**: #F5F7FA (light text), #B0BCC9 (secondary text)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (primary text)

**Emotional Intent**: Approachable, trustworthy, student-focused - shows growth and learning potential.

### Layout Paradigm
- **Simple Hero Section**: Name, brief intro, quick links
- **Straightforward Sections**: Home → About → Projects → Contact
- **Card-Based Layout**: Easy to scan and understand
- **Sticky Navigation**: Always accessible

### Signature Elements
1. **Simple Typography**: Clean, readable fonts
2. **Subtle Hover Effects**: Gentle interactions
3. **Minimal Icons**: Only where necessary
4. **Consistent Spacing**: Professional but not corporate

### Interaction Philosophy
- **Smooth Transitions**: 200ms for all interactions
- **Subtle Feedback**: Hover states are gentle
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized for quick page loads
- **Mobile-First**: Works great on all devices

### Animation
- **Minimal Motion**: Only essential animations
- **Entrance Effects**: Fade-in as sections load
- **Hover States**: Subtle scale and color changes
- **No Distractions**: Animations support, not distract

### Typography System
- **Display Font**: "Poppins" (700 weight) for headings - modern and clean
- **Body Font**: "Inter" (400-500 weight) for text - highly readable
- **Monospace**: "Fira Code" (400 weight) for code - technical feel
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size differences for scanning

---

## Design Decisions
This approach prioritizes **clarity and authenticity**. It's professional enough for an academic assignment but personal enough to show Dilani's individual style. The dark blue theme is sophisticated without being intimidating, and the simple layout makes it easy for visitors to understand who Dilani is and what skills she has.
